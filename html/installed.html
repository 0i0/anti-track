Welcome to anti-track

anit-track is a browser extension to limit the amount of data that you leak while surfing it is by default configured to remove the cookies which are send to google when you search while loggedin to gmail

the way it works is that it is configurde to blacklist sites using regular expressions you can than easy the blacklisting by providing whitelist filters

for example:
black list all domains matching the word: google. (this include google.com, www.google.de, mail.google.com , etc)
than you can white list a subset of this by providing the white list filter mail.google. this include (mail.google.com, mail.google.de etc)
than if the host remains on the black list all cookies are removed for the request
